Ham Sandwich Recipe

Description

Make and eat in less than 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Slices of white/brown bread
  • Slices of black forest ham
  • 100g of sliced cheese
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • 1 bottle of mustard
  • 1 jar of mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Place down 2 slices of bread for each sandwich on the working surface.
  2. Peel off a leaf of iceberg lettuce.
  3. Place iceberg lettuce leaf and sliced tomato onto slice of bread.
  4. Place 2 slices of black forest ham on both slices of bread.
  5. Using a butter knife, spread the mayonnaise on one slice of bread.
  6. Apply mustard to the slice of bread.
  7. Close the sandwich and serve immediately.
