Lasagna Recipe

Description

How to make lasagna that is simple to make and delicious to eat!

Ingredients

  • 10 oz. lasagna noodles
  • 1.5 lbs of lean ground beef
  • 3 cups of cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • garlic powder
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp of parsley

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Using a pot filled with water, bring it to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 10 minutes then drain.
  3. Using a skillet, cook ground beef until it is brown. With a fork, mix the cottage cheese, eggs, garlic powder, and parsley. With a separate bowl, combine the tomato sauce and tomato paste
  4. With a large (9x13 inch) baking tray, layer noodles, ground beef, tomato sauce mixture and cottage cheese mixture. Repeat the process to create another layer. Cover with tin foil and bake for 30 minutes. With 8 minutes remaining, remove the tin foil.
