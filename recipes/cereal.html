Cereal Recipe

Description

Start your breakfast with this simple meal!

Ingredients

  • 250 ml of milk
  • Bag of preferred choice of cereal
  • 1 break cereal bowl
  • 1 table spoon

Steps

  1. Pour cereal into the cereal bowl to just reach the inside edge of the bowl.
  2. Pour the milk into the bowl of cereal so almost all the cereal is submerged.
  3. Using the table spoon, mix the cereal and milk and serve immediately.
